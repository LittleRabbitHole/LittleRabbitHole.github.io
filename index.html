Welcome to Ang’s Website

Hello, my name is Ang Li. I am an experienced quantitative researcher with a Ph.D. in Information Science, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction and data science. With over 2 years of industry expertise, I've successfully led research projects focused on user understanding and metric development. At Spotify, I played a pivotal role in leading impactful research initiatives, collaborating with cross-functional teams of data scientists, researchers, and engineers.

My research demonstrates versatile applications, encompassing the development of metrics to assess the impact of various recommendation interventions, the delivery of insights for pinpointing limitations and offering suggestions for immediate next steps, and the provision of valuable insights to inform the design of recommendation strategies. I am eager to continue contributing insights and expertise to dynamic projects in the intersection of research, data science, and technology.

Before I joint Spotify, I finished my PhD from University of Pittsburgh, School of Information Science, advised by Dr. Rosta Farzan. . During my PhD, I also worked closely with Dr. Yu-Ru Lin, and the PICSO LAB.
