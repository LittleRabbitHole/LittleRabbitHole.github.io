Welcome to Ang’s Website

This is Ang Li, a third year PhD student in the program of Informaion Science and Technology at School of Information Science, University of Pittsburgh. My advisor is Dr. Rosta Farzan.

Research Interest

My research at the University of Pittsburgh lies in the field of social computing and computational social science to understand the user behaviors in the online communities, to design more effective systems and support their users in achieving their goals. In my research studies, I use both qualitative and quantitative research methods such as content analysis, statistical analysis, and data mining techniques. I am highly motivated with studies that can facilitate the activities in the online communities, improve users’ experience, and hence make such communities sustainable.
