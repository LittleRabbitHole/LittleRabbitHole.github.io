DePaul University, Class CSC521 Monte Carlo Simulation Final Project

Simulated the stock portfolio, and managed its risk based on Value at Risk. Based on the past 250 days stocks’ log returns and current stocks’ prices, Resampling and Cholesky Decomposition Methods were performed to first simulate future stocks’ log returns and then compute their prices.
